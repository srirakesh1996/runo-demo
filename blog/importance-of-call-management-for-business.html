Why Call Management Matters for Every Business

Effective call management is a fundamental aspect of running a successful business in today’s fast-paced and customer-centric world. It involves efficiently handling inbound and outbound calls, optimizing communication channels, and ensuring seamless interactions with customers. 

By implementing a robust call management solution, businesses can streamline their operations, enhance customer service, and drive revenue. In this article, we will explore the importance of call management and delve into five key reasons why businesses need a comprehensive call management solution.

What is Call Management?

Call management refers to the systematic handling and organization of inbound and outbound phone calls within a business or organization. It involves efficiently managing various aspects of call handling, such as call routing, call recording, call analytics, and integration with customer relationship management (CRM) systems. 

By implementing a best call center solution, businesses can optimize their communication processes, enhance customer service, and streamline their overall operations. Effective management ensures that businesses can handle calls promptly, provide personalized customer experiences, and maximize productivity.

What are call management features?

Call Recording and Tracking

Call recording and call tracking allows businesses to capture and store audio recordings of phone conversations for quality assurance, compliance, training, or dispute resolution purposes.

Analytics & Reporting

Analytics provides valuable insights and metrics about call volume, call duration, call outcomes, and other key performance indicators (KPIs). This data helps businesses measure and improve their call-handling processes and make informed decisions.

CRM Integration

Integration with customer relationship management (CRM) systems enables seamless access to customer information during calls, helping agents personalize interactions, update customer records, and manage sales pipelines more effectively.

Auto Dialer

An auto dialer automates the outbound calling process by automatically dialing phone numbers from a pre-defined list. It saves time, increases efficiency, and allows agents to focus on engaging with customers.

AI Call Summary

No need to replay long call recordings anymore. With AI call summaries, you instantly get the key points of every interaction. It saves you time and keeps your team focused on what matters most.

Real-Time Dashboards

Stay on top of your team’s performance without waiting for reports. Real-time dashboards give you instant insights into calls, activity, and results. Clear visuals make it easy to track progress at a glance.

Business Call Management Challenges

78% of customers expect businesses to be proactive in every aspect, including business call management. Therefore, mobile phones are not just a communication channel. Instead, they are a communications platform.

Customers have moved on from using laptops and are willing to buy quickly through their mobile phones.

SMEs are constantly looking for ways to improve customer experience. If your business manages inbound and outbound calls, the following are some of the challenges you face daily:

  • Lack of budget for establishing a dedicated call center
  • Without technical support, agents are unable to handle customer calls effectively
  • Customers expect personalized services from businesses
  • Customers want to resolve issues quickly, and they are not interested in waiting
  • Customers don’t like their calls to be transferred to different people to resolve issues
  • Customers prefer to get immediate answers to their questions through WhatsApp or SMS
  • Businesses have to deal with IT issues when a call center is involved
  • Integrating customer appointments with agent calendars requires complex CRM integration
  • Agents can’t provide customer service on the go with desktop-based CRM.

The call center agents would try to resolve customer queries and reroute the calls to specialists when they couldn’t fix them independently.

Even when computer systems are used for telemarketing data, agents have to manually input the data, which can result in human errors. Customer information is not readily available when agents take incoming calls. This outdated model is not suitable for the modern mobile-oriented business world anymore.

2. Business Call Recording

You can record customer conversations directly using the call management solution for business purposes. In addition, it helps you to provide better customer service as you can store the call details easily even when the sales team is out of the office.

3. Inbuilt CRM

The mobile app-based call management solution has built-in CRM, which is greatly helpful for agents to derive value from each call made.

There is no need for manual entries. The CRM platform makes it easy to manage agent calendars, collaborate with peers, fetch customer information, and complete the CRM workflows using a smartphone only.

4. Integration with External CRM

The mobile call management solution integrates seamlessly with external CRM for dedicated outbound call services. Your agents can seamlessly manage inbound and outbound calls using the same smartphone.

Such a solution benefits SMEs that can’t afford a separate department for lead generation and sales. Your sales team can perform both activities using their phones and connect to external CRM when required.

5. Principal Call Management Features

Business call management is not just about calling customers. Agents must understand the user requirements to provide valuable solutions to their problems. It makes it easy for them to contact you when they want to buy something.

A comprehensive call management solution can easily manage all these aspects.

6. Integration with WhatsApp And Email

Business WhatsApp is now gaining popularity as large enterprises want to engage with their customers personally. SMEs can also combine CRM with WhatsApp using mobile app-based call management.

You can call your leads and engage with them on WhatsApp, forging a personal relationship. Using multiple platforms such as WhatsApp, SMS, Email, etc., for communication, develop a strong customer relationship.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is call management important?

Call management is important because it ensures efficient handling of inbound and outbound calls, leading to improved customer service, increased sales, and reduced operational costs. 

It allows businesses to optimize call routing, monitor and analyze call data, enhance agent performance, and deliver exceptional customer experiences.

What is call management in a call center?

In a call center, call management refers to the systematic handling and organization of incoming and outgoing calls. It involves various processes such as call routing, call recording, call monitoring, call analytics, and integration with CRM systems. 

The goal of call management in a call center is to streamline operations, optimize agent performance, and provide high-quality customer service.

Why is call handling important?

Call handling is important because it shapes the first impression customers have of your business. A well-handled call means faster issue resolution, happier customers, and more closed deals. Poor handling, on the other hand, can lead to missed opportunities and lost trust.
