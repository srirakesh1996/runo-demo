What Makes Runo the Most Trusted SIM Based Call Management CRM for 3500+ Businesses?

More Connects. More Conversations. More Wins.

Unlike typical cloud telephony systems, Runo uses SIM-based calls that connect through standard mobile networks, dramatically reducing call drops and increasing the chances your prospects will pick up. Because calls come from real mobile numbers, trust and engagement grow, giving sales teams a powerful advantage to turn more dialed calls into meaningful conversations—and ultimately, more closed deals.

Your Personal AI-Powered Sales Trainer.

Every call is instantly reviewed by smart AI, evaluating how your team sounds, what they say, and the outcome. This insight empowers managers to deliver tailored feedback, recognize effective techniques, and quickly identify coaching moments. With AI supporting your sales coaching, performance improvement becomes measurable and repeatable, helping reps reach their full potential faster.

Follow-Ups That Work—Every Time.

Forgetting to follow up means missed opportunities. Runo’s intelligent reminders automatically alert your team exactly when to reconnect with a lead, based on previous interactions and timelines. This keeps your pipeline active and ensures no potential customer falls through the cracks, making consistent engagement effortless and boosting sales momentum.

Real-Time Visibility to Keep Your Team Ahead.

Sales moves fast, and Runo gives you live insights into call activity, team performance, and response times as they unfold. This immediate data lets managers step in proactively, recognize patterns, and support reps on the spot—without waiting for daily or weekly reports—so the team stays agile and driven toward hitting targets.

A CRM Your Team Will Actually Use Daily.

Runo removes the hassle of juggling multiple tools by combining calling and CRM features in a single, easy-to-use platform. Sales reps naturally keep data updated because everything they need to make calls, log notes, and track progress is right at their fingertips. This seamless experience leads to better data quality, improved sales tracking, and a disciplined pipeline—all without extra effort.

Start Selling Quickly—No Tech Headaches.

Get your team calling prospects within half an hour—no extra equipment, no waiting for IT. Runo’s simple setup means you can import your contacts and launch your campaigns almost immediately. This smooth onboarding saves valuable time and allows sales teams to focus on what matters most: closing deals from day one.
